Welcome to Team 15's Web App

Hello! Welcome to our app! Please log in to view navigational links.

Once logged in, click the links above to move throughout the site.

  • Home takes you back here
  • Your Page takes you to your page, where you can post messages, and get your URL for sharing your page (Note: you can visit other user pages if you have their URL)
  • About Our Team tells you a little bit about us
  • Chart lets you view a fun chart relating happiness to freedom
  • Map is an interactive map where you can place map markers for other users to see
  • Site Community is a list of all active users on the site
  • Message Feed shows all messages that have been posted to the site
  • Site Stats diaplays some statistics about the site
  • Logout will log you out of the site

This site is designed to help people find other people to hang out with! We hope to help you make some super meaningful connections, and make the world a better place in the process!
